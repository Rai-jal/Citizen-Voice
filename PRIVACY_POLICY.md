# Privacy Policy for CitizenVoice

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## 1. Introduction

CitizenVoice ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
- Email address
- Name (if provided)
- User-generated content (reports, fact-check claims, comments)

### 2.2 Device Information
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- IP address

### 2.3 Usage Information
- App interactions and navigation patterns
- Features accessed
- Content viewed

### 2.4 Permissions We Request
- **Microphone:** For voice input and audio attachments in reports
- **Camera:** For photo attachments in reports
- **Photo Library:** For selecting images to attach to reports
- **Location:** For location-based reporting (optional)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain our services
- Process and manage reports and fact-check submissions
- Send important service-related notifications
- Improve user experience and app functionality
- Analyze usage patterns for service enhancement
- Ensure security and prevent fraud

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party cloud service provider
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information
- Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data in the following circumstances:
- **Service Providers:** With trusted third-party service providers (e.g., Supabase, OpenAI for AI features)
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law or to protect our rights
- **Public Reports:** Reports you submit may be made publicly visible after admin approval (unless marked anonymous)

## 6. AI Services and Data Processing

Our app uses OpenAI services for:
- AI chatbot responses
- Audio transcription (Whisper)
- Text translation

When using these features:
- Audio and text data is sent to OpenAI for processing
- We do not store your voice recordings long-term
- OpenAI's privacy policy applies to data processed by their services

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and associated data
- Request data portability
- Withdraw consent (where applicable)

To exercise these rights, contact us at: [your-email@citizenvoice.app]

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
